# Nivo Investor Deck – Messaging & Structure Tightening Plan

Objective:
Improve clarity, tighten messaging, and slightly reduce overall length (~10–15%) while preserving the current positioning, tone, and most of the existing wording.

Focus areas:
- Stronger and clearer investment proposition
- More explicit "Why now" thesis
- Reduced repetition
- Improved investor readability
- Consistent formatting
- Slight length reduction without changing substance

Important constraint:
- Keep management CVs unchanged
- Preserve overall structure of the deck
- Prefer editing existing text rather than rewriting


------------------------------------------------------------
1. OVERALL STRUCTURAL ADJUSTMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------

Goal: improve flow and remove repetition.

Current sections that overlap slightly:

- Opening proposition
- Opportunity
- Investment overview
- Nordic Compounder Model

1. Opening proposition

3. Opportunity

5. Value creation

Add a **new short section after the opening**:

3–5 bullet points summarising the proposition.


------------------------------------------------------------
2. OPENING SECTION – TIGHTEN MESSAGE
------------------------------------------------------------

Current opening text is good but slightly long.

Current:

> We acquire profitable Nordic SMEs with strong products, loyal customers and proven business models — but where operational potential remains untapped.

> Many of these businesses operate with manual workflows, fragmented systems and limited operational transparency.

> The underlying business is sound; the execution can be significantly improved.


Suggested tightening:

Keep structure but reduce repetition.

Replace the three opening paragraphs with:

We acquire profitable Nordic SMEs with strong products, loyal customers and proven business models — but where operational potential remains untapped.

Many of these companies operate with manual workflows, fragmented systems and limited operational visibility. The businesses themselves are sound; execution can be significantly improved.

Nivo combines hands-on ownership with proprietary internal tooling for sourcing, analysis and operational upgrades. This allows us to identify opportunities faster, analyse companies systematically and implement improvements more effectively.


------------------------------------------------------------
3. ADD "WHY NOW" CLARITY
------------------------------------------------------------

The deck currently implies this but does not state it strongly.

Add a short paragraph near the beginning of **The Opportunity** section.

Insert:

### Why now

A large share of Nordic SMEs were built before modern digital tools became widely adopted. Many remain profitable but operationally under-digitised.

At the same time, practical AI and modern SaaS infrastructure now allow smaller companies to upgrade systems, pricing, reporting and operational workflows quickly and cost-effectively.

This creates a narrow window where operational improvements can unlock disproportionate value.
