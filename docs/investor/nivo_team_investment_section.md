# Why Invest in Nivo -- Team Capability

## Deal sourcing and transaction execution

Together the team has extensive experience in corporate finance and
venture investing, including the execution of **more than 80 equity
capital markets transactions and over 20 IPOs**. The team has also
deployed **over €50 million in venture investments**, including early
involvement in companies such as **iZettle and Readly**, with iZettle
later acquired by **PayPal**. This experience provides strong expertise
in **valuation, capital raising, transaction structuring and exit
strategy**.

## Operational value creation

The team combines investment experience with hands-on operational
leadership. Together the team has **built and scaled a €60+ million
direct-to-consumer business within Electrolux**, growing the Nordic
ecommerce channel from zero to **€30 million in revenue**. This provides
direct experience in **digitalisation, ecommerce strategy, international
expansion and revenue optimisation**, capabilities that are highly
relevant for many of the companies Nivo intends to acquire.

## Financial leadership and governance

The team also brings strong financial leadership experience from
**listed companies and international organisations**, including building
finance functions, managing reporting and overseeing capital allocation.
This ensures Nivo can implement **institutional-grade financial
discipline, governance and reporting across portfolio companies from the
outset.**

## Entrepreneurial and founder perspective

Finally, the team combines institutional experience with
entrepreneurship, having **founded and invested in multiple companies**,
including technology platforms in fintech and digital infrastructure.
This provides a strong understanding of **founder-led businesses and the
operational realities faced by small and mid-sized companies.**

Together, these capabilities allow Nivo to operate as an **active owner
capable of sourcing proprietary opportunities, accelerating growth and
executing successful exits.**
